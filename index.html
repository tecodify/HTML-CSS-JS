Frequently Asked Questions

Where does the interactive platform take place?

Telegram serves as the dynamic interactive platform, enabling round-the-clock interaction with your instructor. Additionally, we provide practical platforms to enhance and refine your ethical hacking and cybersecurity skills effectively.

Will there be materials given to students?

Yes, of course, materials will be provided during training, which includes self-explanatory content, video tutorials, step-by-step instructions for required software, access to practical platforms for practice, and completion of practical projects before advancing to the next course.

Are Bugfreak certificates recognized?

The experiences and the skills you posses from Bugfreak training are worth being recognized by any employer around the world.

Who is the founder of Bugfreak?

Bugfreak is a collective effort led by a team of Cybersecurity experts with diverse backgrounds and extensive expertise of our team which allows us to deliver exceptional cybersecurity training to our students.


Who are the instructors?

We have a team of highly qualified and experienced tutors who are experts in the field of Cybersecurity. Our tutors possess a diverse range of backgrounds, and industry experience, allowing them to bring real-world knowledge into the training.

What is the enrollment process?

We'll send you an email after clicking the registration link on the first page to register and make a payment. Someone will attend to you on Telegram to create your interactive class and assign an instructor to begin your cybersecurity training—all within a few minutes of enrollment.
